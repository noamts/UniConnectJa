U of Waterloo

The University of Waterloo is based in Waterloo, Ontario on nearly 1000 acres of land. The university
is well known for the co-op program which is one of the largest in the world with over 20,000
students enrolled. The university is also well known for its computer science, engineering,
and mathematics programs.
