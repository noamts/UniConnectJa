Uni Connect a JA company

Giving students guidance for their academic future, through connections and personalized information.

Uni Connect will allow you to connect and interact with current university students in Ontario to find the best school and program for you.

About Us

Uni Connect is a small startup created by high school students in the JA Company Program whose sole purpose is to provide
other high school students with the information that they need to provide a smooth transition into university.
Our main avenue is through providing a secure and simple method of providing an in depth Q and A on any question that a future
university student might have. This is done through an indirect approach, the high school student fills out a form with their
preferred university and program as well as one or more questions that they would like answered by a student in this program.
We will direct this to said student, and they will send us a video response. At this point the high school student
will then be given the video on payment of a small fee of which the majority will go to the university student in payment.
This process is as secure as the software we used.
