Nipissing

Nippissing is a small liberal arts university located near Lake Huron and in the heart of nature.
Nippissing is known for its small student body of just over 5000, and small ratio of students to faculty.
Nippissing offers over 25 different programs and is home to 7 different varsity sports teams including soccer,
basketball and volleyball. Nippising was founded in 1992 and has a great relationship with the community of North Bay.
