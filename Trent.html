Trent

Trent University is a unique university that has two locations, the main campus is located in Peterborough, Ontario,
and the other one is located in Durham allows for people of all locations to attend Trent. Trent University is known
for its real world experiential programs as well as its small class sizes to allow for great discussions and maximized learning.
Trent prides itself on rigorous programs with all the benefits of high-quality research and interactive teaching within a personal,
close-knit community.
