Mc Master

McMaster has been a consistently top ranked university in Canada due to its high quality programs
and upholding prestige. McMaster has been home to three Nobel Prize winners, tech innovators,
and top industrialists. Its diverse environment and professional proficiency makes McMasters one
of the best universities to attend in Canada.
McMaster is the home of the largest research nuclear reactor in the Commonwealth.
