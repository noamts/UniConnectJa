Lakehead

Lakehead University is located in Thunder Bay Ontario with a smaller campus located in Orillia.
Lakehead is known for its advanced computer science program and its unique location in Western
Ontario in the heart of nature. Lakehead has many different clubs and committees you can join such as
PC Gaming Society and Ski and Snowboard Club, as well as many varsity sports such as Basketball,
Volleyball and Nordic Skiing.
