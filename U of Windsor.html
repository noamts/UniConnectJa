Windsor

The University of Windsor is located in Windsor Ontario, a short drive to the US city of Detroit and a three and a half hour drive to Toronto.
The University of Windsor was created in 1963, it was created from four colleges Essex College, Canterbury College, Iona College and Holy
Redeemer College and incorporated into the University of Windsor. This university offers over 200 undergraduate programs with specializations in acting, media and film.
