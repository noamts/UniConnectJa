Guelph

The University of Guelph is a university located in Guelph,Ontario. It is one of the top
comprehensive universities in Canada and was established in 1964. Through the collaboration of
Humber College, The university of Guelph-Humber which is located in Toronto,Ontario offers a
special applied learning that allows students to obtain both a university degree and college
diploma. The University of Guelph is known for its excellence in arts and sciences. It is mainly
known for Canada's Food University. The University of Guelph is ranked top 20 in Canada and top
500 in the world. This university offers 94 undergraduate degrees, 48 graduate programs and 6
associate degrees. University of Guelph provides workplace experience in all of their programs
which makes it exciting to learn.
