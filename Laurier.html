Laurier

Wilfrid Laurier University has been a school of high academic excellence since 1911. Laurier is
located mainly in Waterloo, Ontario, but has two other campuses in Brantford, Ontario and
Kitchener, Ontario. Laurier offers 90 programs and is home to around 19,000 students. Students are
encouraged to apply their knowledge they learn to their everyday lives and is what the Laurier
experience is all about. This can be applied through co-op programs and community service, then
using these real-life experiences back in the classroom. Innovative programs range from arts and
social sciences, social work, science, business and economics. Laurier's acceptance rate is 89% but
will vary depending on the program. As well, the tuition, class sizes and entry requirements vary
depending on the program which is desired.
