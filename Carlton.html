Carlton

Carleton University is located in Canada’s capital: Ottawa. It is between the Rideau canal and river,
close to the Quebec border. The top programs at Carleton are journalism, public affairs,
international affairs, architecture and technology. While it varies for different programs, a minimum
average of 70% is required and certain programs require portfolios, auditions and supplementary
applications. With over 300 clubs and societies, Carleton has many opportunities for involvement
and community building. The Ottawa location brings many advantages such as parliamentary
work opportunities and skating on the Rideau during the winter.
