University of Western Ontario

The University of Western Ontario, founded in 1878, is ranked 8th in Canada, and 211 worldwide.
It's located in London Ontario, a two hour drive from Toronto. It is home to 3 affiliate universities:
Huron, Brescia and Kings. Western is known for its exceptional academic programs,
breakthrough research discoveries and outstanding student experience. Western has a wide variety of options,
appealing to all types of students with more than 400 undergraduate programs and 88 different graduate degree programs.

Some of the most populat programs at western are Business, Dentistry, Education, and Engineering.
The average Tuition is 7,576.74 CAD and there is an average employment rate of 95.2%.
