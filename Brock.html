Brock

Brock University is located in Saint Catherines Ontario, a short drive to Niagara Falls and an hour
drive to Toronto. Brock prides themself on their strong student satisfaction rating, and amazing
mental health services. Brock has 100+ unique programs including, A BBA International Double
Degree, Criminology and so many more. Brock also offers experiential learning inside and outside
the classroom, Brock has over 20 different types of invaluable opportunities built into our courses.
As well, Brock has a strong sports presence with over 25 varsity sports.
This University was named adter Major-General Sir Isaac Brock.
