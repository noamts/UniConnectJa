York

York University is located in Uptown Toronto. York has eight colleges that make up the university,
these include Bethune College, Calumet College, Founders College, McLaughlin College,
New College, Stong College and Vanier College. York is a leading institution in education and science
and is known for its unique international locations. When studying at York you will have many opportunities
to have hands-on experience. York prides itself on its high job rates for alumni, with 92% of students
getting a job two years after graduation. York has a strong student life with over 130 clubs and committees.
